How to Play

1. Call Lightning: The board is dark! Click "Call Lightning" to briefly reveal the letters. Pay close attention before they fade back to stone!

2. Spell Words: Drag letters from the board down into the tray slots to spell a word.

3. Reveal: Click "Reveal" to check your word. Valid words score points based on length:
• 2-3 letters: 1 point
• 4 letters: 4 points
• 5 letters: 6 points
• 6 letters: 8 points

Clear the board to win a 50-point bonus!
